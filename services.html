Services

Practical, senior-level support

Four ways we partner with leaders.

01 / Fractional CPO

Fractional Chief People Officer

For growing organizations that need executive-level people leadership without a full-time hire.

We embed as part of your leadership team, typically one to two days per week, to own the people agenda alongside the CEO. From talent strategy and organizational design to compensation, performance, and culture, we bring the senior-level judgment your business needs at a fraction of the cost of a full-time executive.

Typical deliverables:

  • People strategy aligned with business priorities
  • Talent planning, hiring, and onboarding systems
  • Performance management and compensation frameworks
  • Executive coaching for the CEO and leadership team

02 / Leadership

Leadership Development

For leaders ready to grow, and for the organizations investing in them.

Effective leadership is learned, not assigned. We work one-on-one with executives and in small cohorts with leadership teams to build the skills, habits, and self-awareness that distinguish strong leaders from competent managers.

Typical engagements:

  • 1:1 executive coaching
  • Leadership team offsites and workshops
  • New-leader onboarding and development
  • Manager training programs

03 / Culture

Culture Transformation

For organizations whose culture isn't yet matching their ambitions.

Culture is the product of what leaders do consistently, not what's written on a poster. We help organizations define the culture they actually need to win, then build the practices, expectations, and accountability structures that make it real.

Areas of focus:

  • Cultural assessment and diagnostic
  • Values, norms, and operating principles
  • Accountability and performance practices
  • Change management and rollout

04 / Organization

Organizational Consulting

For leadership teams working through structure, communication, or operational alignment.

Sometimes the people work is really an organizational design problem: roles that don't fit, structures that no longer scale, or communication patterns that slow everything down. We help leadership teams diagnose what's actually getting in the way and chart a clear path forward.

Engagements may include:

  • Organizational structure and role design
  • Operating cadence and communication review
  • Cross-functional alignment
  • Change planning and execution support

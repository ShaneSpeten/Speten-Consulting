Meet Shane Speten

People & Business Executive with 15+ years of director-level experience leading transformational growth (+$1B P&L), developing high-performing leaders, and building people-first cultures across Fortune 500 organizations including Amazon, Lowe's, LG, Sears, and Pella.

Shane's background spans director-level leadership roles across sales, operations, talent development, customer experience, and strategic initiatives within retail, supply chain, consumer services, repair, and home improvement industries. He specializes in aligning people strategy with business strategy to strengthen leadership capability, improve engagement, drive organizational effectiveness, and deliver sustainable business results.

Throughout his career, he has led enterprise initiatives focused on leadership development, employee engagement, coaching, succession planning, recognition, and culture transformation. He has led organizations of 250+ professionals while partnering with executive leaders to drive scalable growth, operational excellence, and values-based leadership.

Known as a culture builder and people champion, Shane believes exceptional business performance starts with investing in people, developing strong leaders, and creating environments where teams thrive.

  • Designed and launched Sears' first national coaching program across 2,000 stores and 20,000 leaders, contributing to a 10% increase in sales productivity and $900M in incremental revenue.
  • Co-created a national career pathing program for 12,000 consultative associates that reduced turnover by 30%.
  • Led digital transformation and coaching initiatives across 1,600+ stores and 12,000 retail leaders in partnership with senior executives.
